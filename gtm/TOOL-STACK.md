## Part 6: What NOT to Waste Time On

1. **Don't self-host everything at once.** Start with free SaaS tiers. Only self-host when you hit limits.
2. **Don't set up Mautic.** Complex PHP setup. Brevo covers everything for now.
3. **Don't build a CRM** until >50 leads. Spreadsheet, then Twenty.
4. **Don't use Copy.ai/Jasper/Writesonic.** You have Claude. They're redundant and worse.
5. **Don't deploy Upptime** before paying users exist.
6. **Don't automate LinkedIn posting** beyond scheduling. LinkedIn bans automation aggressively.
7. **Don't build custom video pipelines** until you've exhausted CapCut + Gamma + Runway free tiers.
8. **Don't self-host analytics** while PostHog cloud free tier (1M events/mo) covers you.
9. **Don't use Hotjar.** Microsoft Clarity is better AND unlimited AND free.
10. **Don't deploy BigBlueButton** until you're running regular webinars. Jitsi or Google Meet works for 1:1 demos.

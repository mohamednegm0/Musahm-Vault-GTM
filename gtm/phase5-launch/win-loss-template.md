## تعليمات الاستخدام | Usage Instructions

1. **One entry per deal.** Complete a Win or Loss analysis for every beta sign-up that reaches a terminal state (activated or expired/declined).
2. **Fill during or immediately after.** Do not batch entries at month-end; details decay.
3. **Quote the champion.** Whenever possible, capture the champion's actual words about why they activated or did not. Direct quotes are more valuable than summaries.
4. **Monthly review.** At each month-end, complete the Summary Dashboard and share with product + marketing. Patterns in win/loss data should directly inform messaging updates, feature prioritization, and sales playbook adjustments.
5. **Confidentiality.** This document contains client-specific data. Do not share externally. Store in a governed Vault workspace with restricted access.

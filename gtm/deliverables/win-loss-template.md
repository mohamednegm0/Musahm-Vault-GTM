## Quarterly Review Process

1. **Aggregate all win/loss records** from the quarter.
2. **Calculate win rate** by sector, entity type, deal size, and competitor.
3. **Identify top 3 win themes** and top 3 loss reasons.
4. **Share with product team:** Any feature gaps that caused 2+ losses become product roadmap candidates.
5. **Share with marketing team:** Win themes inform positioning adjustments and case study priorities.
6. **Share with sales team:** Update battle cards with new competitive intelligence.
7. **CEO brief:** One-page quarterly win/loss summary with trends and recommended actions.

## Interview Guide (Post-Decision)

Use these questions during a 20-minute call with the buyer after the deal closes.

**For Wins:**

1. What was the deciding factor?
2. What almost made you choose someone else?
3. Which feature sealed the deal?
4. Would you recommend Musahm to a peer? What would you say?

**For Losses:**

1. Can you share what ultimately drove the decision?
2. What did the winning vendor offer that we did not?
3. Was there something we could have done differently during the sales process?
4. Would you consider Musahm for a future initiative?

**For No-Decision:**

1. What caused the project to stall?
2. Is the need still there, or has the priority shifted?
3. What would need to change for you to revisit this?

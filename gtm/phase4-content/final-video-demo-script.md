# Product Demo Video Script: Musahm Vault

## Duration: ~2:40 (160 seconds) | Arabic VO + English subtitle cards

---

## Tone and Style Guide

| Aspect | Direction |
|--------|-----------|
| **Video type** | Product demo / screen walkthrough |
| **VO style** | Male or female professional Arabic narrator. MSA with natural Khaleeji rhythm. Think: senior product manager presenting to a board. Warm, confident, unhurried. NOT a tech startup pitch. NOT a news anchor. |
| **Music** | Minimal electronic underscore. Quieter than the brand video. Never competes with VO. Light rhythmic pulse during screen interactions. Resolves cleanly at CTA. |
| **Visual style** | Screen captures of actual Vault UI (Arabic RTL interface), with subtle motion graphics overlays: callout highlights, pointer animations, transition wipes. Brand palette: #1F2937 navy for UI chrome, #c3924d gold for highlights and callouts, #f9fafb backgrounds. |
| **Subtitles** | English subtitle cards throughout. Max 12 English words per card. Timed in SRT to match VO pacing. |
| **Aspect ratio** | 16:9 primary (YouTube, LinkedIn, website). 9:16 vertical cut for social stories. |

> **Companion piece:** This demo video complements the 78-second brand video (`video-script.md`). The brand video answers "What is this?" This video answers "How does it actually work?"

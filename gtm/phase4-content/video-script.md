# Product Video Script: Musahm Vault Launch

## Duration: ~78 seconds | Bilingual (Arabic VO + English subtitle cards)

---

## Tone & Style Guide

| Aspect            | Direction                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Brand concept** | Concept 3: KSA-Native-Tech ("Built Saudi"). Confident national pride, not startup energy.                                                                                                                                       |
| **Overall tone**  | Warm, confident, unhurried. An institutional product introduction, not a tech ad. The voice of a Saudi company that has already won.                                                                                            |
| **VO style**      | Male or female professional Arabic narrator. MSA with natural Khaleeji rhythm. Calm authority.                                                                                                                                  |
| **Music**         | Clean, minimal electronic underscore. No drums until the product reveal. Builds subtly. Think: corporate documentary, not tech ad.                                                                                              |
| **Visual style**  | Mix of motion graphics (product UI animations) and live-action-style renders (modern Saudi office environment). Clean, bright, professional. Palette: #1B4332 dark green, #c3924d gold, #2563EB oasis blue, #f9fafb background. |
| **Subtitles**     | English subtitle cards throughout (max 12 words per card). Arabic caption overlay for key statistics.                                                                                                                           |
| **Aspect ratio**  | 16:9 primary (YouTube/LinkedIn), 9:16 cut for social stories/reels                                                                                                                                                              |

> **Subtitle card rule:** No single card exceeds 12 English words or 10 Arabic words. At 150 WPM subtitle reading speed, each card lasts 3–6 seconds for comfortable reading.

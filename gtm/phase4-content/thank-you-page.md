1. **اجمع محاضر اجتماعات المجلس** من آخر 12 شهراً. ستكون أول ما ترفعه إلى مساحة "اجتماعات المجلس."

3. **راجع صلاحيات الوصول الحالية:** من يصل إلى وثائق الحوكمة اليوم؟ هذه القائمة ستصبح هيكل الصلاحيات في Vault.

**EN:**
**Prepare your documents for the transition:**

1. **Gather your board meeting minutes** from the last 12 months. These will be the first documents you upload to the "Board Meetings" workspace.
2. **Identify 3-5 core users** who will start using Vault: Board Secretary, Legal Counsel, CFO.
3. **Review current access permissions:** Who accesses governance documents today? This list becomes your Vault permissions structure.

## Design Notes

- **Tone:** Warm, confident, action-oriented. The user just committed. Reward the decision.
- **Layout:** Clean single-column. No sidebar distractions. No upsell. This page exists to build confidence.
- **Colors:** White background, `#1B4332` header bar, `#c3924d` accent on the timeline step numbers.
- **No navigation menu:** Remove the main site nav. The user should focus on next steps, not click away.
- **Mobile:** Full-width layout. Timeline stacks vertically. Resource links use large touch targets (min 44px).
- **Analytics:** Fire a `vault_beta_signup` conversion event on page load. Track clicks on each resource link.

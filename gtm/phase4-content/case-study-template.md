# Case Study Templates: Musahm + Vault

> **Framework:** CHIC (Challenge / Hypothesis / Implementation / Change)
> **Purpose:** Three case study formats for sales collateral, proposals, and marketing.
> **Important:** All templates contain placeholder fields. The hypothetical example is clearly labeled and does not represent real client data.
> **Date:** 2026-04-02

## CHIC Framework

Every Musahm case study follows the CHIC arc. This maps to how KSA governance buyers think: they had a problem they could not ignore, they bet on an approach, the platform was deployed, and the numbers changed.

| Phase              | What It Answers          | Content Focus                                                                                                              |
| ------------------ | ------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **C**hallenge      | "What was broken?"       | Specific governance pain: scattered docs, failed audits, no chain of custody, regulatory exposure                          |
| **H**ypothesis     | "What did they bet on?"  | Why this company chose Musahm over alternatives. The decision logic, the evaluation criteria, who championed it internally |
| **I**mplementation | "How did it happen?"     | Setup timeline, migration scope, which features activated, who was involved, any friction encountered                      |
| **C**hange         | "What is different now?" | Before/after metrics, operational shifts, regulatory outcomes, team behavior changes                                       |

### Writing Rules for CHIC

1. **Challenge must be specific.** "Document management was hard" is unusable. "The CFO kept board resolutions on his personal laptop and CMA found zero version control during a routine review" is a case study.
2. **Hypothesis section names the alternatives rejected.** If they considered SharePoint, Majles, or Ebana, say so. The reader wants to know why those lost.
3. **Implementation must include a timeline.** "Week 1: migration. Week 2: permissions. Week 3: workflows live." Readers gauge feasibility by timeline.
4. **Change must be quantified.** If the client cannot provide numbers, use directional claims: "response time dropped from weeks to hours" or "the team stopped using email for document circulation entirely."

##### C: التحدي / The Challenge

شركة الأساس العقارية تدير 12 مشروعاً عقارياً نشطاً في الرياض وجدة. مجلس الإدارة يجتمع شهرياً، واللجان الثلاث (تدقيق، ترشيحات، مخاطر) تجتمع كل شهرين. قبل مساهم، كانت محاضر الاجتماعات تُكتب في Word وتُرسل بالإيميل. العقود مع المقاولين كانت على أجهزة المدير المالي الشخصية. وعندما طلبت هيئة السوق المالية وثائق حوكمة محددة أثناء مراجعة دورية، استغرق الفريق أسبوعين لتجميعها.

Al-Asas Real Estate manages 12 active development projects across Riyadh and Jeddah. The board meets monthly, and three committees (Audit, Nominations, Risk) meet bimonthly. Before Musahm, meeting minutes were written in Word and circulated via email. Contractor agreements lived on the CFO's personal devices. When the CMA requested specific governance documents during a routine review, the team needed two weeks to compile them.

##### H+I: الحل والتنفيذ / The Solution & Implementation

فعّلت الشركة مساهم لإدارة اجتماعات المجلس واللجان والتصويت الإلكتروني، ثم أضافت Vault لإدارة الوثائق. تم ترحيل أكثر من 2,000 وثيقة إلى مساحات العمل المحوكمة الست خلال الأسبوع الأول. سجل المساهمين ربط ملفات 47 مساهماً بسجلاتهم في المنصة مباشرة.

The company activated Musahm for board and committee meeting management and e-voting, then added Vault for document management. Over 2,000 documents were migrated to the six governed workspaces during the first week. The Shareholder Registry connected 47 shareholders' files directly to their platform records.

## Client Interview Question Guide

Use these 10 questions to gather the information needed to populate any of the three formats above. Conduct the interview in the client's preferred language. Record and transcribe if possible.

### القسم الأول: الخلفية / Section 1: Background

**1. أخبرنا عن شركتكم: القطاع، الحجم، نوع الكيان القانوني.**
Tell us about your company: sector, size, legal entity type.

_Populates: Client name, sector, company size, entity type._

**2. كيف كنتم تديرون وثائق الحوكمة قبل مساهم؟ أين كانت تُحفظ؟ من كان يصل إليها؟**
How were you managing governance documents before Musahm? Where were they stored? Who had access?

_Populates: The Challenge section, current state before Musahm._

**3. ما أكبر مشكلة واجهتكم في إدارة الوثائق؟ هل يمكنك ذكر حادثة محددة؟**
What was the biggest problem you faced in document management? Can you describe a specific incident?

_Populates: The Challenge section, specific pain point and anecdote._

### القسم الثاني: الحل / Section 2: The Solution

**4. لماذا اخترتم مساهم و Vault تحديداً؟ هل قيّمتم بدائل أخرى؟**
Why did you choose Musahm and Vault specifically? Did you evaluate alternatives?

_Populates: The Solution section, decision rationale and competitive context._

**5. ما المزايا التي تستخدمونها يومياً؟ وما أكثر ميزة أحدثت فرقاً في عملكم؟**
Which features do you use daily? Which feature made the biggest difference?

_Populates: Key Features Used list._

**6. كيف كانت تجربة التهيئة والترحيل؟ كم استغرقت؟**
How was the setup and migration experience? How long did it take?

_Populates: The Solution section, implementation details._

### القسم الثالث: النتائج / Section 3: Results

**7. ما النتائج القابلة للقياس التي حققتموها؟ (وقت، تكلفة، عدد وثائق، سرعة استجابة)**
What measurable results have you achieved? (Time, cost, document count, response speed.)

_Populates: Key Metrics table, before/after numbers._

**8. كيف تغيّر أداءكم في التدقيق أو المراجعات التنظيمية بعد استخدام المنصة؟**
How has your performance in audits or regulatory reviews changed after using the platform?

_Populates: Results section, regulatory/compliance improvement._

### القسم الرابع: الشهادة / Section 4: Testimonial

**9. لو أردت أن توصي مساهم لزميل في شركة أخرى، ماذا ستقول في جملة واحدة؟**
If you wanted to recommend Musahm to a colleague at another company, what would you say in one sentence?

_Populates: Client Quote (all three formats)._

**10. هل توافقون على نشر اسم شركتكم وشعارها واقتباسكم في مواد مساهم التسويقية؟**
Do you agree to publish your company name, logo, and quote in Musahm marketing materials?

_Populates: Publishing permission status. Critical; do not publish without explicit written consent._

## Interview Best Practices

- Schedule 30 minutes. Most interviews take 20.
- Send the questions in advance so the client can prepare numbers (Question 7 especially).
- Record the call (with permission) and transcribe. The best quotes come from natural speech, not prepared statements.
- Ask follow-up questions when the client mentions specific numbers or incidents. "You said two weeks -- can you walk me through what that looked like day by day?"
- After the interview, send a draft of the case study for client review before publishing. Allow 5 business days for approval.
- If the client is uncomfortable with specific numbers, offer ranges ("reduced response time by more than 90%") or anonymization ("a Saudi real estate company with 80+ employees").

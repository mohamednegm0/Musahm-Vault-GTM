### Lead Paragraph

Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, [⚠️ NEEDS CLIENT INPUT: Date]. Musahm, the Saudi corporate governance platform, announced the beta launch of **Vault**, its document management system. Built from scratch as a native extension of the Musahm platform, Vault enables Saudi companies to manage their governance documents in one governed, audit-ready system.

---

### The Problem

Saudi companies manage their governance documents, board meeting minutes, general assembly resolutions, contracts, policies, across fragmented systems. Minutes live in email threads. Contracts sit on personal devices. Policies are stored in shared drives where no one knows which version is current.

When an auditor requests a specific document, there is no single source of truth. The search takes days instead of seconds. There is no record proving who uploaded a document, who modified it, or who approved it.

Foreign document management systems available in the market were designed for general storage. They do not understand Saudi governance structures, do not support Arabic as a primary language, and do not connect documents to their corporate context.

---

### The Solution: Vault by Musahm

**Vault** is a document management system built by Musahm for Saudi corporate governance. It is governed from the ground up, with permissions, audit trails, and workflow controls built into every layer.

**Six governed workspaces ready from day one:**
Board Meetings, Association Meetings, Committee Meetings, Decisions, Contract Documentation, and Policies & Regulations. Each workspace has independent permissions and its own workflow configuration.

**Five permission levels:**
Viewer, Commenter, Editor, Organizer, and Admin. Access control operates at the individual document level. Each level defines precisely what a user can see and do.

**Complete audit trail:**
Every action is logged with user identity, timestamp, and details: uploads, edits, deletions, views, and shares. No deletion without a trace. No edit without a preserved previous version.

**Secure external sharing:**
Documents shared with external parties (lawyers, auditors) through one-time password (OTP) access with a watermark bearing the recipient's name. The document is displayed, not sent.

**Intelligent search:**
Keyword search and AI-powered field extraction. Advanced semantic search and Ask Vault are under development and will be available in an upcoming release.

**Automated workflows:**
Approval and review chains before any document is published. Nothing is finalized without passing through the defined approval path.

---

### Shareholder Registry, A Differentiator

What separates Vault from other document management systems is its direct integration with the Shareholder Registry in Musahm. Each shareholder's documents, agreements, contracts, resolutions, are linked to their registry profile, not stored in an isolated folder. This connection places every document in its proper corporate context and simplifies audit and review processes.

---

### Context

Vault launches at a time of accelerating corporate governance development in Saudi Arabia. The new Companies Law has raised documentation requirements for all companies, including unlisted and limited liability entities. Saudi companies need tools built for their reality: tools that support Arabic as a primary language, understand local governance structures, and provide audit trails consistent with regulatory expectations.

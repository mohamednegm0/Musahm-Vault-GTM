## Cold Email Scoring Rubric

Every email in this document scores against four dimensions. Emails below 60/100 get rewritten before sending.

| Dimension       | 25 pts                                                  | 18 pts                                   | 10 pts                   | 0 pts                            |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------ | -------------------------------- |
| **Relevance**   | Sector-specific pain + named regulation                 | Sector-specific pain, generic regulation | Generic governance pain  | No clear connection to recipient |
| **Pain**        | Quantified cost of inaction (days, SAR, audit findings) | Described cost without numbers           | Implied cost             | No pain articulation             |
| **Proof**       | Named client or verifiable metric                       | Anonymized case reference                | Product capability claim | No proof                         |
| **CTA Clarity** | Single ask, specific time frame, low commitment         | Single ask, no time frame                | Multiple asks            | No CTA or buried CTA             |

## Prospecting: Hunter.io Email-Finding Instructions

### Real Estate

Search by company domain on Hunter.io. Target titles: أمين سر مجلس الإدارة (Board Secretary), مدير الامتثال (Compliance Director), مدير الشؤون القانونية (Legal Director). Saudi real estate companies often list board members on Tadawul or the Ministry of Commerce registry. Cross-reference with LinkedIn Sales Navigator using "عقارات" + "حوكمة" as keywords.

### Healthcare

Search hospital group and medical holding domains. Target: مدير الحوكمة (Governance Director), المستشار القانوني (Legal Counsel), مدير الجودة (Quality Director). Healthcare companies licensed by the Ministry of Health publish leadership on their websites. Use Hunter.io domain search on group-level domains (not individual hospital sites).

### Education

Search university and educational group domains. Target: أمين عام مجلس الأمناء (Secretary General of Board of Trustees), مدير الشؤون الإدارية (Administrative Affairs Director). Saudi educational institutions list leadership teams on their websites and the Ministry of Education portal. Use Hunter.io with `.edu.sa` domain filters.

### Family Holdings

Family offices rarely have public domains. Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator to find: مدير مكتب العائلة (Family Office Director), المستشار القانوني (Legal Counsel), المدير التنفيذي (CEO). Cross-reference with the Ministry of Commerce corporate registry for holding company names, then search those domains on Hunter.io. Expect lower hit rates; supplement with WhatsApp introductions through mutual connections.

Family holdings and offices manage sensitive documents (shareholder agreements, inheritance plans, family constitutions, and subsidiary governance records) across generations. Documents live in lawyers' offices, personal safes, and scattered drives. When succession events occur or disputes arise, there is no single governed source of truth. The Companies Law now requires formal documentation even for unlisted family companies, and many are not ready.

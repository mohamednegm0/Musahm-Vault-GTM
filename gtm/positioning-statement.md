# Musahm Canonical Positioning Statement

> **Framework:** Positioning Canvas (FOR / WHO / OUR / UNLIKE / WE PROVE IT BY)
> **Date:** 2026-04-02
> **Status:** Canonical -- all GTM content must align to this statement.

---

## The Statement

### English

**FOR** Board Secretaries, CEOs, CFOs, and Legal Counsel at Saudi SMBs (20-500 employees) -- closed joint-stock, listed, and LLC entities --

**WHO** manage corporate governance across WhatsApp groups, email chains, and shared drives, and face rising compliance pressure from the new Companies Law, CMA requirements, and Vision 2030 governance mandates,

**OUR** platform, Musahm, is the only Saudi-built governance platform that combines board management, shareholder registry with SMS communications, e-signatures, and a governed document management system (Vault) in a single Arabic-first product.

**UNLIKE** Majles.tech (board management only, no shareholder registry, no DMS), Ebana (standalone DMS, no governance context), SharePoint (general-purpose storage, no governance logic), and Diligent (enterprise pricing, English-first, no Saudi regulatory alignment),

**WE PROVE IT BY:**
- Three Saudi companies operating on the platform today: Al-Ufuq (education), Miral (healthcare), Amwaj (education).
- The only Saudi platform with an integrated shareholder registry and direct SMS shareholder communications.
- Six pre-configured governance workspaces with five-level access control (Viewer to Admin) and per-document ACLs.
- Complete audit trail on every action -- user identity, details, and timestamp.
- OTP-verified external document sharing with watermarks.
- AI-powered field extraction from documents. Keyword search across all fields. Semantic search and Ask Vault (RAG Q&A) under active development.

## Usage Rules

1. **Every piece of GTM content must be traceable to this statement.** If a claim appears in content that cannot be traced back to one of the positioning elements above, it needs justification or removal.

2. **Do not add features to the proof points without verifying them in the product.** The proof points listed above are verified against the codebase as of 2026-04-02.

3. **The "UNLIKE" section is a living competitive map.** Update it when competitors ship new features or enter new markets.

4. **Derivative formats are approved for use as-is.** No need to rewrite the one-liner or competitive positioning for standard use cases. Customize only for specific personas or verticals.

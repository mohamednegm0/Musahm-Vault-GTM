#### Objection 4: "هل التوقيعات الإلكترونية معتبرة قانونياً في المحاكم السعودية؟" / "Are your e-signatures legally valid in Saudi courts?"

**Response framework:**

Yes. Musahm's electronic signatures comply with نظام التعاملات الإلكترونية (the Saudi Electronic Transactions Law), which grants electronic signatures the same legal standing as handwritten signatures when the system meets the technical requirements specified in the law.

Every signature in Musahm carries a full audit trail: signer identity, timestamp, and document version at time of signing. This chain of evidence is designed to be presentable in regulatory proceedings and dispute resolution.

We are not offering a generic e-signature tool adapted for Saudi Arabia. Musahm was built for Saudi corporate law. The e-signature module is architected around the Electronic Transactions Law's requirements from the ground up.
